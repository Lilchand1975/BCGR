DHAMAKA 2018

Bhutanese Community of Greater Richmond (BCGR-VA) has recently announced about a cultural musical event to be held on Nov. 4th 2018 in Richmond. The board further announced that Mr. Rajesh Payal Rai, popular Nepali singer, is invited for the event. A 25-member adhoc organizing team has made to work on the management of the event. The team is excited to arrange necessary details for the event. The organizing team will further update the progress on the preparation of the event. The intention of the event is to mark the special occassion of festival "Dashain" and "Dipawali". It is also intended to relieve hard working bhutanese american people in Richmond VA. Events like this will not only unite the community together, but also teaches newer generation about our unique culture.

An approach is made to forecast participation in unique cultural events with long gestation periods. Experimental observations of choice are derived for conjoint measurement type multiattribute alternatives that describe possible event configurations. The choice data are analyzed by means of discrete choice econometric models. This platform will provide an opportunity to local artists to showcase their talents before a huge crowd. A multinomial logit choice model is applied to forecast the choice of attendance at various types of international expositions. The empirical work was part of the planning efforts undertaken to investigate the potential demand for an international exposition to be held in Eastern Australia in conjunction with the Australian 1988 bicentennial celebrations.
